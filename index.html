Courier Services

Alset Transit LLC provides reliable local courier support for businesses, professionals, and organizations throughout the Minneapolis–St. Paul metro area.

Medical Document Courier

Secure transport for non-patient medical paperwork, clinic forms, records, and business-related healthcare documents.

Legal Document Courier

Delivery support for law offices, court-related documents, signed forms, contracts, and time-sensitive paperwork.

Business & Office Courier

Professional pickup and delivery of envelopes, small parcels, office supplies, keys, files, and other business items.

Same-Day Local Delivery

Metro-area delivery options for scheduled, on-demand, and time-sensitive courier needs.
